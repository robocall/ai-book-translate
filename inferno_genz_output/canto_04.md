*   **Hyperbole & Exaggeration:** Gen Z often uses over-the-top expressions to emphasize a point.
*   **Relatable References:** Linking the characters and their sins to modern behaviors (simping, energy vampires, obsession with money) makes it more accessible.
*   **Emphasis on Tone & Atmosphere:** Capturing the overall sense of dread and despair – the “anxiety” and “major shade” – is key.
*   **Short, Punchy Sentences:** Reflecting a common characteristic of Gen Z communication.
*   **Direct Address:** "Fam" - A word used to address a group of people.
