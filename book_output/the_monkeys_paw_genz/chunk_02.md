Okay, so suddenly this sergeant-major is dropping this whole monkey’s paw thing – like, a dried-up monkey paw with a spell on it. Apparently, some ancient dude, a fakir, made it to show how fate’s a total jerk and messing with it is a one-way ticket to disaster. Three guys can each get three wishes, which is both terrifying and kinda tempting. Herbert, being Herbert, immediately wants to wish for a bunch of them. The whole room gets hyped up, and Morris is trying to play it cool, but the White family is *obsessed*. It’s getting seriously weird, and you can just *feel* the tension building.
