Okay, so things are *seriously* messed up. Mr. and Mrs. White are basically trapped in a horror movie now, fueled by this cursed monkey’s paw. The old man’s trying to be the voice of reason, but his wife is spiraling, convinced she can just wish Herbert back. It’s like, she’s actively hunting for a way to bring him back, even though it’s clearly a terrible idea. Then, BAM, the house starts acting up – creaky stairs, a freaky mouse, and the candle just *dies*. And then this insane knocking starts. It’s like the house is trying to tell them something, but they’re too busy being obsessed to listen. She’s screaming about it being Herbert, running to the door, and the old man is desperately trying to stop her, convinced it’s a ghost. It’s a full-blown panic, and the stranger is just *watching* from the shadows, adding to the whole unsettling vibe. Honestly, this is peak anxiety.
