Okay, so things are spiraling HARD. The visitor from “Maw and Meggins” drops the bombshell – Herbert’s dead, caught in a machine. Like, seriously tragic, but the kicker is the whole “no responsibility” thing and a measly £200 payout. Mr. White, predictably, loses it completely, face-plants to the floor, and Mrs. White is just… staring. It’s like the universe is actively trying to make them miserable, and honestly, it’s succeeding.
