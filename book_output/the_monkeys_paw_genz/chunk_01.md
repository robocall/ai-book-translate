Okay, so the Whites are chilling in their ridiculously isolated villa, playing chess with a seriously intense dad and a slightly cooler son. The grandma’s knitting, the wind’s howling, and Mr. White is already stressing about how awful their location is – basically, it’s a swampy nightmare. Then, this super intense Sergeant-Major Morris shows up, bringing whiskey and a whole vibe of “let’s just relax.” It’s awkward, a little weird, and Mr. White’s already got that guilty-grin thing going on, sensing something’s about to go *wrong*.
