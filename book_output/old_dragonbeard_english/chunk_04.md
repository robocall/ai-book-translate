Li Jing, advised by Liu Wenjing, prepared to travel to Taiyuan to investigate rumors of unusual activity. He was instructed to meet Zhang at the Fen Yang Bridge the following morning, anticipating the arrival of the Sui Emperor, who was traveling incognito and exhibiting an altered demeanor. Upon arrival in Taiyuan, the Emperor, disguised as the Curly-Bearded Guest, immediately recognized Liu Wenjing and, despite his unsettling appearance and cryptic pronouncements, confirmed his identity as the Emperor.  Liu Wenjing, impressed by the Emperor’s apparent sincerity and authority, expressed his admiration, while Zhang remained observant and cautious. The encounter concluded with Zhang’s assessment that the Emperor’s situation was nearing a definitive resolution, and he requested that Li Jing also meet him.
