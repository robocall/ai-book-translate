Li Jing’s journey continued with the assistance of Liu Wenjing, culminating in a lavish reception orchestrated by the Curly-Bearded Guest. Following their return to Chang’an, Li Jing was invited to a substantial gathering at a residence associated with the Guest. The event was characterized by extravagant displays – a large reception hall filled with servants, musical performances by twenty young women, and an opulent feast. The Curly-Bearded Guest presented Li Jing with a considerable sum of money, intended to facilitate his endeavors, explaining that he had previously spent twelve years engaged in military service. He emphasized the importance of securing a position and offered the funds as a means to achieve this. The gathering culminated in a display of wealth and spectacle, designed to impress and potentially influence Li Jing.
